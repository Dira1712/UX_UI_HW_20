I've always been captivated by the intersection of creativity and functionality.
In my journey from engineering to fine art painting, and eventually to interior design, my innate curiosity and passion for art and design has fuelled my diverse career path.

From the precise calculations of engineering to the free-flowing expression of fine art painting, I discovered that each discipline offered unique perspectives on problem-solving and creative expression. As I delved deeper into the world of interior design, I found myself drawn to the intricate details and the transformative power of thoughtful design in enriching people's lives.

However, while building my services as an interior designer, I realized that crafting meaningful experiences extended beyond physical spaces-it encompassed digital spaces as well. This realization led me into the world of UI/UX design. Here, I discovered a perfect amalgamation of artistry and functionality. UI asks how to visually communicate a solution,while UX delves into how people interact with that solution. In the realm of design, there's a graceful balance between aesthetics and usability, where every pixel and interaction is meticulously considered to harmonize with the user experience.

In UI/UX design, I found my true passion - a space where creativity intertwines with empathy, and where design is not just about solving problems but also about understanding people and their needs. It's a symbiotic relationship where people inform design, and designs inform people. Product design is a realm filled with boundless opportunities to craft experiences that deeply connect with users on a profound level.
